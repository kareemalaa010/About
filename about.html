About

Uni-Net is a platform that provides multiple services at the same time; which are Social, Sessions and Study.

Here we connect students with teachers and professors by mixing the idea of social (by being active on our platform making connections and uploading Academic posts and sharing knowledge to other students) and studying (by attending sessions and solving quizzes) with a certified points system that could be used later by your Educational Institute to be added to your GPA or Grades.

Students compete in getting more points so they get higher rank on the platform which will give them more benefits, special badges and allow them to post more Academic and Educational contents with no limits to what type of content they post(Terms and conditions apply) so the other students could use the higher rank students’ pdfs, files or quizzes to get better and study harder.

Sessions

Sessions is a virtual interactions or meetings conducted over the Internet.

Sessions can be Made / Host In 5 ways:
1- Faculty Members.
2- Professors.
3- assistant professor.
4- university team leader / HR.
5- star student.

Sessions Attendance System:
- When a Professors /Assistant Professor Starts a Session, you have 15 minutes to join or else you can not enter and will count as an absence.
- When you enter a lecture, a timer starts and to count as an attendance you need to be there for more than 90% of the lecture time after the 15 minutes have passed.

Session Contains:
- Assignment (you can submit it before the lecture)
- Quizzes (you can take it at the end of the lecture)

Social Media

This platform enables users to participate in social networking by creating academic content, sharing their thoughts on any academic subjects, commenting on other users’ content, and reposting it.

The social media System:

- There are ranked Accounts and their order is: The Uni Account, professor, Assistant Professor, Uni Team Leader, Star Student, Student.

- Any post that is not related to an academic subject will be deleted and could give the account owner(user) ban from using social media and many more features

- You can not have multiple accounts only the account given by the Learning Institution

- Any swearword or insults or Harsh words are not accepted and could result in getting a ban

Points System

Points-System, points will give you Rewards and Ranks by interacting with our website and progressing in your Academic studies. Earning points: There are 4 main ways to gain points:

Through Social media:
- Posting any academic posts.
- Reposting posts.
- Getting Recognition (like, comment, repost) by Professors /Assistant Professors.

Through Sessions:
- Attending Lectures.
- Submitting Assignments.
- Getting Average / Above Average grade in an Exam.
- Bonus points From Professors /Assistant Professors.

Through GPA:
- Scoring Above Average GPA.

Through University Teams:
- Register in a competition made by University Teams.
- Winning / Being in the top 5 ranks of this competition made by University Teams.
